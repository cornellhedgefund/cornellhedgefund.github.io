About Us

The Cornell Hedge Fund is the first and foremost student-run undergraduate hedge fund at Cornell. Currently, we are the largest undergraduate finance club at Cornell with over 75 students across many majors.

We strive to bring more opportunities from the world of finance to the Cornell community. We believe that students who wish to break into finance should not be limited to specific majors and years. We strive to prepare students for careers in finance through the following ways:

Education

Members are educated in the fundamental methods of finance through our robust education program and weekly education series

Experience

We provide equal opportunities for all majors and backgrounds to gain real investing experience through an array of investing strategies

Opportunity

The Cornell Hedge Fund presents opportunities to network with top finance employers several times throughout the year
