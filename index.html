The Cornell Hedge Fund

The first and foremost student-run undergraduate hedge fund at Cornell

The Cornell Hedge Fund is the first and foremost student-run undergraduate hedge fund at Cornell. Currently, we are the largest undergraduate finance club at Cornell with over 75 students across many majors. As a finance club, we strive to enrich our members with education, experience, and opportunities to better prepare them for careers in finance.
