The Cornell Hedge Fund

The first and foremost student-run undergraduate hedge fund at Cornell

The Cornell Hedge Fund is Cornell's largest undergraduate investment organization. Through real-world investing experience, ongoing education, and mentorship, we seek to provide students with the opportunities to develop their skills and interests in finance, regardless of their background. We are committed to our foundational values of integrity, innovation,and diversity to sustain an atmosphere that maximizes our members' personal and professional growth.
